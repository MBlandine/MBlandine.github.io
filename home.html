WHO WE ARE:

Shine And Waves Natural, Inc. is a natural hair movement with the desire to help empower both women and men of African descent from all over Africa and worldwide to embrace their God-given “natural” hair.

We do not only seek to inspire all African women and men to feel confident and sexy, we want to encourage people in all stages of their hair journey to go, or return natural. We do this by sharing pictures of other African people with natural hair, and providing educational and practical lifestyle tips for #Simple Natural Hair Regimen to ultimately find their style and purpose. As our core values are founded on empowerment, going natural is only the beginning. We aim to galvanize African people to become the main agents of development and success in their communities.


Natural is the new niche, and it is our goal to help African women embrace their crown in style because it is exotic, gorgeous, bold and beautiful. We hope that you would join us on this journey to help make a difference, and to help you, be the best you.
